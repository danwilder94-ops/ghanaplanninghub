# Ghana Planning Hub

## A Digital Planning Reference and Decision Support Platform for Ghana

Ghana Planning Hub is a web-based planning reference and decision-support platform developed to improve access to planning standards, zoning requirements, development control guidelines, and spatial planning resources in Ghana.

The platform was developed to support planning professionals, development control officers, consultants, GIS practitioners, researchers, students, and other stakeholders involved in land use planning and development management.

---

## Vision

To contribute to the digital transformation of planning practice in Ghana by providing accessible, practical, and user-friendly planning tools that improve decision-making, consistency, and compliance with planning standards.

---

## Objectives

* Simplify access to planning standards and zoning regulations.
* Support development control and permit review processes.
* Assist planners during community planning and local plan preparation.
* Improve planning education and professional practice.
* Provide a centralized knowledge base for planning information in Ghana.

## Target Users

### Planning Professionals

Support for development control, zoning interpretation, and plan preparation.

### Development & Settlement Planning Students

A learning and reference platform for studio work, assignments, and research.

### GIS and Spatial Analysts

Quick access to planning standards and zoning references for spatial analysis.

### Consultants and Developers

Improved understanding of planning requirements and development standards.

### Metropolitan, Municipal and District Assemblies

Decision-support for planning and development management.
